# Unified Notation Sheet

This sheet consolidates all notation used across the manuscript.  
Notation is structural, not algebraic.

---

## Core Symbols

- **F** — field  
- **M** — motion  
- **C** — curvature  
- **K** — collapse  
- **R** — reconstitution  
- **A** — alignment  
- **S** — stability  

These symbols denote structural phenomena, not variables.

---

## Ecological Conditions

- **O₂** — oxygen (linguistic spaciousness)  
- **H** — altitude (vantage)  
- **E** — ecology (environmental support)  
- **T** — triadic configuration  

These determine whether the field can sustain motion.

---

## Curvature Indicators

- **C⁺** — positive curvature  
- **C⁻** — negative curvature  
- **Cᵒ** — oscillatory curvature  

Curvature reveals the shape of the field.

---

## Collapse Signatures

- **K₁** — tightening curvature  
- **K₂** — oscillatory motion  
- **K₃** — alignment fragmentation  
- **K₄** — dimensional reduction  

Collapse signatures precede collapse.

---

## Reconstitution Pathway

- **R₁** — curvature return  
- **R₂** — widening motion  
- **R₃** — alignment restoration  
- **R₄** — coherence rebuilding  
- **R₅** — stability re-establishment  

Reconstitution is sequential and structural.

---

## Structural Relationships

- **M → C** — motion reveals curvature  
- **C → K** — curvature predicts collapse  
- **K → R** — collapse enables reconstitution  
- **R → S** — reconstitution leads to stability  
- **S → M** — stability supports motion  

These relationships define the geometry of the field.
