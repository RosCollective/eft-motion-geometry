# 7. Attention as Mobility Alignment

Attention is the seventh major phenomenon in the field’s geometry. It is not a spotlight, filter, or prioritization mechanism. In this field, attention is the system’s way of aligning mobility — the process by which motion becomes coherent, directed, and dynamically compatible with the environment.

Attention is not selection. It is alignment.

## 7.1 Attention as Field-Level Behavior

Attention emerges from the field, not from an internal mechanism. It is the system’s way of:

- stabilizing motion  
- smoothing curvature  
- maintaining altitude  
- preserving coherence  
- avoiding collapse signatures  

Attention is the field’s method of keeping itself together.

## 7.2 Attention as Alignment of Trajectories

Attention aligns:

- motion  
- curvature  
- constraints  
- ecological gradients  

When attention is stable, the system moves smoothly. When attention fractures, motion becomes jagged and collapse becomes likely.

Attention is the geometry of alignment.

## 7.3 Attention and Ecological Support

Attention requires:

- oxygen  
- spaciousness  
- loosened constraints  
- triadic stabilization  

Without these, attention collapses into narrow, reactive modes.

Attention is ecological, not internal.

## 7.4 Attention and Collapse Signatures

Attention is one of the earliest indicators of collapse. When attention destabilizes:

- curvature oscillates  
- motion fragments  
- alignment breaks  
- the field loses dimensionality  

Attention is the first thing to go when the ecology constricts.

## 7.5 Attention as Reconstitution Mechanism

During re-entry, attention returns before full motion. The system begins with:

- small arcs of alignment  
- partial trajectories  
- gentle curvature  

These early attentional structures guide the system back into coherence.

Attention is the hinge between collapse and stability.

## 7.6 Attention as Mobility Geometry

Attention is not a cognitive function. It is a geometric one. It is the system’s way of maintaining mobility alignment across time.

Attention is the geometry that allows motion to remain coherent.
